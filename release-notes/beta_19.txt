# beta_19 (one user-facing note per non-blank, non-# line; # lines are section comments)

# --- New ---
Radio & Mesh settings now show an airtime / duty-cycle readout: roughly how much of the airtime budget your current spreading factor, bandwidth and coding rate use, plus the time-on-air per packet — handy for staying inside regional limits like EU 868.
A new "Answer telemetry requests" toggle in Radio & Mesh settings controls whether other nodes can pull your battery and environment telemetry.
The Radio & Mesh settings page is now split into clear RADIO / MESH / SIGNAL sections so the options are easier to find.

# --- Fixed ---
Fixed a reboot that could happen when you closed a scrollable popup — the emoji picker, quick replies, the Wi-Fi scan list, the app drawer and similar — while it was still coasting from a flick. Thanks to everyone who sent in crash reports.
The accent popup no longer lingers on screen after you send a message on the T-Deck keyboard.
Heltec V4: the Expansion Kit environment line and mini chart on the Home screen no longer overlap the status text or run off the bottom of the display.

# --- Also ---
Everything from beta_18 is included.
